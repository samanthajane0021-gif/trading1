Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply create an account with your email, deposit crypto to your wallet, and start trading.

What assets can I trade?

You can trade cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum), forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD), and commodities (Gold, Silver).

Is the platform secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade security. All transactions are encrypted and monitored for fraud.

How do I withdraw my funds?

Go to Portfolio > Withdraw and select your crypto method (BTC, ETH, or USDT). Enter your wallet address and amount.
